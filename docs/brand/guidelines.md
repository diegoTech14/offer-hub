# Brand Guidelines

This document defines the OFFER-HUB brand identity, visual language, and usage guidelines.

## Brand Identity

### Mission Statement
OFFER-HUB empowers marketplaces to provide secure, non-custodial escrow payments without building complex payment infrastructure.

### Brand Values
1. **Trust:** Non-custodial, transparent, and secure
2. **Simplicity:** Easy to integrate, easy to use
3. **Flexibility:** Self-hosted, provider-agnostic
4. **Modern:** Cutting-edge technology and design

---

## Visual Identity

### Logo
The OFFER-HUB logo consists of the wordmark "OFFER-HUB" in a modern sans-serif typeface.

**Primary Logo:** Full wordmark  
**Secondary Logo:** "OH" monogram (for small spaces)

**File Formats:**
- SVG (preferred for web)
- PNG (with transparent background)
- PDF (for print)

---

### Logo Usage

**DO:**
- Use the primary logo whenever possible
- Maintain clear space around the logo (minimum 20px)
- Use on backgrounds with sufficient contrast
- Scale proportionally

**DON'T:**
- Distort or stretch the logo
- Change the logo colors (except approved variations)
- Add effects (shadows, gradients, outlines)
- Place on busy backgrounds

---

### Color Variations

**Primary Logo:** Teal (`#149A9B`) on light backgrounds  
**Inverted Logo:** White on dark backgrounds (`#002333`)  
**Monochrome:** Black or white for special cases

## Typography

### Primary Typeface
**Inter** - Modern, clean, highly readable

**Weights:**
- Regular (400): Body text
- Medium (500): Subheadings
- Semi-Bold (600): Headings
- Bold (700): Hero text

### Fallback Typefaces
Roboto, Outfit, system fonts

### Usage
- **Headings:** Semi-Bold (600) or Bold (700)
- **Body:** Regular (400)
- **Captions:** Regular (400), smaller size
- **Buttons:** Medium (500)

---

## Voice & Tone

### Brand Voice
- **Professional:** Knowledgeable and trustworthy
- **Approachable:** Friendly and helpful
- **Clear:** Direct and jargon-free
- **Confident:** Assured but not arrogant

### Tone Guidelines

**DO:**
- Use active voice
- Be concise and clear
- Explain technical concepts simply
- Provide actionable guidance

**DON'T:**
- Use jargon without explanation
- Be overly casual or informal
- Make promises you can't keep
- Use negative or accusatory language

---

### Example Messaging

**Good:**
> "OFFER-HUB provides secure, non-custodial escrow for your marketplace. Get started in minutes with our simple API."

**Bad:**
> "OFFER-HUB is the best payment orchestrator ever! You'll never need anything else!"

## Imagery

### Photography Style
- **Modern:** Clean, contemporary aesthetics
- **Professional:** High-quality, well-composed
- **Diverse:** Inclusive representation
- **Contextual:** Relevant to payments, marketplaces, technology

### Illustration Style
- **Minimalist:** Simple, clean lines
- **Geometric:** Structured shapes
- **Branded Colors:** Use brand palette
- **Purposeful:** Support content, not decorate

---

## Iconography

### Style
- **Outline:** Line-based, not filled
- **Rounded:** Soft corners, friendly feel
- **Consistent:** Uniform stroke width (2px)
- **Simple:** Clear and recognizable

### Usage
- Use icons to support text, not replace it
- Maintain consistent size within groups
- Use brand colors for active/selected states
- Provide alt text for accessibility

---

## Design Principles

### Neumorphism
OFFER-HUB uses a neumorphic design language with soft shadows and tactile depth.

**Key Elements:**
- Dual shadows (dark + light)
- Consistent light source (145° top-left)
- Soft, rounded corners
- Monochromatic base

See [Neumorphism Guide](../design/neumorphism.md) for details.

---

### Spacing & Layout
- **Grid:** 8px base unit
- **Padding:** Generous whitespace for premium feel
- **Alignment:** Consistent, structured layouts
- **Hierarchy:** Clear visual hierarchy
